Kheer(Desert)

Description

Maggi is a popular brand of instant noodles known for its convenience and quick preparation. Originating in Switzerland but now widely enjoyed around the world, Maggi noodles are a staple in many households, especially in India and Southeast Asia.

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles (wheat flour-based)
  • Seasoning mix (includes salt, spices, and flavor enhancers)
  • Water
  • Optional add-ins: vegetables, eggs, or proteins (e.g., chicken, tofu)

Steps

  1. Boil 1 Â½ to 2 cups of water in a pot or saucepan.
  2. Add the instant noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the noodles for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally until they are soft.
  4. Add the seasoning mix to the pot, stirring well to ensure the noodles are evenly coated with the flavoring.
  5. Continue cooking for another 1-2 minutes until the seasoning is fully dissolved and the noodles have absorbed the flavor.
  6. If desired, add any optional ingredients like vegetables or proteins and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
